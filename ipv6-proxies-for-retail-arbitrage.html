Why IPv6 proxies for retail arbitrage

Retail arbitrage hinges on spotting price and stock gaps across marketplaces faster than competitors, but scraping those catalogs at speed gets your IP blocked quickly. Proxies let you monitor prices and inventory across many stores continuously. Profitable arbitrage runs on undetected, high-throughput data collection.

IPv6 Proxies draw from the enormous, low-cost IPv6 address space. That makes them reasonably well matched to retail arbitrage, where data freshness is what counts.

Advantages for retail arbitrage

  • Enormous IP supply enables aggressive, cheap rotation
  • Lowest cost per IP thanks to IPv6 address abundance
  • Whole subnets available for large-scale distributed jobs
  • Fast datacenter-grade speeds and low latency

Pro tip: Monitor clearance and regional-store pages from local residential IPs, where the deepest markdowns and stock imbalances that fuel arbitrage margins appear first.
