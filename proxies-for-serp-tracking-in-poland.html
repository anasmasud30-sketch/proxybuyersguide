SERP Tracking in Poland

Scraping search engine results pages at scale, across thousands of keywords and multiple locales, hammers Google's anti-bot defenses harder than almost any other task. Proxies distribute those queries so your SERP scraper keeps returning structured results instead of block pages. High-volume SERP data collection is effectively impossible without a rotating IP pool.

Doing this specifically for Poland means you need Polish exit IPs — otherwise Poland targets serve the wrong region’s content or block you outright.

Poland’s consumer internet is served mainly by Orange Polska, Play (P4), and Netia. Popular local targets include Allegro, OLX.pl, Ceneo, and Empik. Allegro dominates Polish e-commerce, making local IPs important for accurate pricing and listing data.
