Why residential proxies for account management

Running multiple accounts on any platform, whether ecommerce, ads, or marketplaces, triggers linking and mass-ban systems the instant those accounts share an IP or device fingerprint. Proxies isolate each account behind its own stable, trusted connection. Safe multi-accounting is built on one clean IP per profile.

Residential Proxies route through real ISP-assigned home IP addresses. That makes them especially well matched to account management, where account survival rate is what counts.

Advantages for account management

  • Extremely high trust and low block rates on protected sites
  • Precise geo-targeting down to country, state, and city
  • Millions of IPs make bans and rate limits hard to enforce
  • Ideal for sneaker, ticketing, and social platforms that filter datacenter IPs

Pro tip: Keep one sticky proxy permanently bound to each account and pair it with an isolated browser profile, since IP consistency signals a stable, legitimate user.
