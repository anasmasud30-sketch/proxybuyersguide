Quick answer

Taiwan proxies route through genuine Taiwanese IPs. For most use cases, residential proxies with Taiwan coverage give the best balance of trust and price.

Why use Taiwan proxies

When a target serves Taiwan-specific content, pricing, or availability, you need a local Taiwanese IP to see what a real user in Taiwan sees — across search results, ad delivery, e-commerce pricing, and streaming catalogues.

Taiwan’s consumer internet is served mainly by Chunghwa Telecom, Taiwan Mobile, and Far EasTone. Popular local targets include PChome, momo, Shopee Taiwan, and Ruten. Taiwan is a dense e-commerce market led by PChome, momo, and Shopee, and uses Traditional Chinese, so local IPs are needed for accurate pricing and localization checks.

Route everything through a single address and you throttle yourself long before the target does.
