Quick answer

Mobile Proxies can work for Expedia, but residential proxies are usually the safer pick against its defences.

Mobile Proxies on Expedia

Expedia personalizes flight and hotel pricing by location, currency, and search history, so fare-aggregation and travel-intelligence tools scrape through geo-matched residential IPs to collect comparable quotes. A rotating residential pool avoids the throttling that hits repeated fare searches from a single address.

Expedia uses price/geo cloaking, request throttling, and bot detection. Mobile Proxies carry a low ban risk here, so pair them with careful pacing or step up to residential proxies.
