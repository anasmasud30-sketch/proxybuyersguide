IPRoyal + Ad-Tech Testing

Ad-tech platforms, DSPs, and publishers must test how creatives, targeting, and bid logic behave across countries, devices, and networks that no single office connection can replicate. Proxies simulate real users in any geography and network type. Rigorous ad-tech QA runs on diverse, authentic IPs.
