Retail Arbitrage in Romania

Retail arbitrage hinges on spotting price and stock gaps across marketplaces faster than competitors, but scraping those catalogs at speed gets your IP blocked quickly. Proxies let you monitor prices and inventory across many stores continuously. Profitable arbitrage runs on undetected, high-throughput data collection.

Doing this specifically for Romania means you need Romanian exit IPs — otherwise Romania targets serve the wrong region’s content or block you outright.

Romania’s consumer internet is served mainly by Digi (RCS & RDS), Orange Romania, and Vodafone Romania. Popular local targets include eMAG.ro, OLX.ro, Altex.ro, and Publi24.ro. eMAG dominates Romanian e-commerce and OLX leads classifieds, so local IPs are needed to see accurate pricing and listing availability across the country.
