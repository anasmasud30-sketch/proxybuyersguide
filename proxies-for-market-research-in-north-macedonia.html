Market Research in North Macedonia

Credible market research means seeing products, pricing, promotions, and content exactly as consumers in each target region do, not as a bot or a foreign visitor. Proxies unlock authentic local views across every market you study. They turn geo-fragmented web data into a coherent competitive picture.

Doing this specifically for North Macedonia means you need Macedonian exit IPs — otherwise North Macedonia targets serve the wrong region’s content or block you outright.

North Macedonia’s consumer internet is served mainly by Makedonski Telekom, A1 Macedonia, and Telekabel. Popular local targets include Pazar3.mk, Reklama5.mk, Time.mk, and Grouper.mk. North Macedonia has effectively two mobile network operators (Makedonski Telekom and A1), and Pazar3 is the main local classifieds target.
