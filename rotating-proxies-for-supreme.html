Quick answer

Rotating Proxies can work for Supreme, but datacenter proxies are usually the safer pick against its defences.

Rotating Proxies on Supreme

Supreme's weekly Thursday drops reward speed over heavy anti-bot defenses, so many coppers run Supreme bots on fast datacenter proxies to submit checkouts in milliseconds across dozens of tasks. Rotating a pool of quick datacenter IPs avoids the per-IP throttling that kicks in during the drop rush.

Supreme uses request throttling, cart/checkout timing checks, and per-IP limits. Rotating Proxies carry a low–medium ban risk here, so pair them with careful pacing or step up to datacenter proxies.
