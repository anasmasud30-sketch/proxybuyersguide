Smartproxy SOCKS5 proxies overview

SOCKS5 refers to a proxy protocol rather than an IP source, tunneling any kind of TCP or UDP traffic at a lower level than HTTP proxies. Because it does not interpret or rewrite your data, SOCKS5 handles everything from web browsing and torrents to gaming, streaming, and email with support for authentication and UDP. It can run over residential, datacenter, or ISP IPs depending on the provider.
