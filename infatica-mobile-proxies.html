Infatica mobile proxies overview

Mobile proxies route traffic through IP addresses issued by cellular carriers to real 3G, 4G, and 5G devices. Because carriers share a small number of IPs across thousands of subscribers via CGNAT, these IPs are almost impossible to ban without collateral damage, giving them the highest trust score of any proxy type. They are the premium choice for the most aggressively protected platforms.

Infatica positions itself as “Balanced mid-market residential and mobile proxies”, with 15M+ residential IPs and subscription pricing from ~$8-12/GB (residential).
