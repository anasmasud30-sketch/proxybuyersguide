DataImpulse + Real Estate Data

Property portals hold rich listing, pricing, and history data behind aggressive anti-scraping defenses and regional access controls. Proxies let real estate analysts and investors collect listings at scale from any market. Comprehensive property datasets depend on rotating, geo-targeted IPs.

DataImpulse brings very low per-GB pricing with no forced monthly commitment and ethically sourced residential pool with global coverage, which maps well to real estate data.
