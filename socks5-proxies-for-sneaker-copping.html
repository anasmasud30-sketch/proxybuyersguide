Quick answer

SOCKS5 Proxies tunnel any TCP or UDP traffic at the protocol level, making them a workable fit for sneaker copping.

Why SOCKS5 proxies for sneaker copping

Limited-release sneaker drops sell out in seconds, and retailers cap orders per IP while banning bot-like traffic outright. Proxies let copping bots submit many checkout attempts from distinct residential addresses simultaneously. In the sneaker game, IP diversity directly determines how many pairs you hit.

SOCKS5 Proxies tunnel any TCP or UDP traffic at the protocol level. That makes them reasonably well matched to sneaker copping, where checkout success rate is what counts.

Advantages for sneaker copping

  • Handles any protocol, not just HTTP and HTTPS traffic
  • Supports UDP for streaming, gaming, and VoIP
  • Lower overhead since payloads are not parsed or altered
  • Built-in authentication for secure, private access

Pro tip: Prioritize low-latency ISP proxies geolocated near the retailer's servers for drops, since speed to checkout beats raw pool size when stock lasts seconds.
